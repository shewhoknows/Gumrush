# Fitness Plan

## Goals
- Build overall strength and endurance
- Improve flexibility and mobility
- Maintain consistency with 4 workouts per week

---

## Weekly Schedule

| Day       | Focus                  | Duration |
|-----------|------------------------|----------|
| Monday    | Upper Body Strength    | 45 min   |
| Tuesday   | Cardio + Core          | 40 min   |
| Wednesday | Rest / Active Recovery | —        |
| Thursday  | Lower Body Strength    | 45 min   |
| Friday    | Full Body + HIIT       | 50 min   |
| Saturday  | Flexibility / Yoga     | 30 min   |
| Sunday    | Rest                   | —        |

---

## Workouts

### Monday — Upper Body Strength
- Push-ups: 3 × 15
- Dumbbell rows: 3 × 12 each side
- Overhead press: 3 × 10
- Bicep curls: 3 × 12
- Tricep dips: 3 × 12
- Plank: 3 × 45 sec

### Tuesday — Cardio + Core
- 20 min steady-state run or cycling
- Bicycle crunches: 3 × 20
- Leg raises: 3 × 15
- Russian twists: 3 × 20
- Mountain climbers: 3 × 30 sec

### Thursday — Lower Body Strength
- Squats: 4 × 12
- Romanian deadlifts: 3 × 10
- Lunges: 3 × 12 each leg
- Glute bridges: 3 × 15
- Calf raises: 3 × 20

### Friday — Full Body + HIIT
- Burpees: 4 × 10
- Jump squats: 4 × 12
- Push-ups: 3 × 15
- Kettlebell swings: 3 × 15
- Box jumps: 3 × 10
- 10 min cool-down walk

### Saturday — Flexibility / Yoga
- Full-body stretching routine
- Hip flexor and hamstring stretches
- Shoulder and chest openers
- Breathing and relaxation

---

## Nutrition Guidelines
- Eat protein with every meal (chicken, fish, eggs, legumes)
- Stay hydrated: 2–3 liters of water per day
- Prioritize whole foods; limit processed snacks
- Post-workout meal within 30–60 minutes

## Progress Tracking
- Log workouts weekly
- Take measurements monthly
- Reassess goals every 8 weeks
